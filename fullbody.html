Full Body Workout

A full-body workout is a training session that targets all major muscle groups in one workout, as opposed to splitting them into different days like an "upper body day" or "leg day". This approach uses compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and push-ups to work multiple areas at once, making it an efficient way to build strength, endurance, and balanced muscle development. It's suitable for both beginners and experienced athletes and often requires fewer sessions per week, typically three with rest days in between.

  • Push-ups – 3 sets of 15
  • Squats – 3 sets of 20
  • Plank – 1 minute
  • Burpees – 2 minutes
