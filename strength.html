Strength Training

Strength training is a type of exercise that involves working your muscles against resistance to build strength, size, and endurance. This resistance can come from various sources, including your own body weight, free weights, resistance bands, or weight machines. It is also known as resistance training or weight training.

  • Dumbbell curls – 3 sets of 12
  • Bench press – 3 sets of 10
  • Deadlift – 4 sets of 8
  • Shoulder press – 3 sets of 12
