Cardio Training

A cardio workout, or cardiovascular exercise, is any activity that elevates your heart rate and breathing for a sustained period. It involves the rhythmic movement of large muscle groups and increases oxygen use, which strengthens the heart and lungs, improves overall health, and burns calories. Examples of cardio include running, swimming, cycling, and dancing.

  • Jumping jacks – 3 minutes
  • Running in place – 5 minutes
  • High knees – 2 minutes
  • Skipping rope – 3 minutes
